HTML:Image

The <img> element represents an image. Sometimes something can be more clearly stated in graphical form, for example as a flowchart, a diagram, a graph, or a simple map showing directions.

The image given by the src attribute is the embedded content, and the value of the alt attribute is the img element's fallback content.

<p>Consider for a moment their logo:</p>
<p><img src="w3c.png" alt="It is written W3C by a white character."></p>
